About BITS Pilani

BITS Pilani is a globally acclaimed institution known for its innovation-driven approach to education. For over 60 years, it has consistently nurtured exceptional talent, producing professionals who go on to lead at some of the world's top consulting and strategy firms, including McKinsey & Company, BCG, Bain, EY-Parthenon, Deloitte, and Kearney. The institute's legacy of excellence continues to shape industry leaders across the globe.

About Us

Founded in 2012, BITS Pilani Consulting Club has established itself as a premier consulting organization, recognized for excellence in strategic problem-solving and delivering impactful solutions. Our core belief is "Strategize. Solve. Succeed." - empowering businesses and individuals through data-driven insights, strategic thinking, and innovative solutions that drive real-world impact.
